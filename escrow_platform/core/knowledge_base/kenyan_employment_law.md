# Key Kenyan Employment Law Provisions (Employment Act 2007)

## Contracts
- Section 9: All contracts must specify nature of work and remuneration
- Section 10: Freelancers entitled to clear payment terms

## Payment
- Section 17: Timely payment required (within 30 days of invoice)
- Section 19: Unpaid work can seek full compensation plus interest

## Dispute Resolution
- Section 41: Parties may agree to alternative dispute resolution
- Section 44: Fair hearing required before any payment adjustment
- Section 87: Digital contracts legally binding if both parties consent

## Termination
- Section 35: Either party may terminate with reasonable notice
- Section 40: Work completed must be compensated regardless of termination

## Key Principles
1. "Substantial completion" entitles full payment unless clear breach
2. Client bears burden of proof for quality claims
3. Any penalty clauses must be explicitly agreed upfront
