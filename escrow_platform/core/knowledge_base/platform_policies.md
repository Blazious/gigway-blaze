# GigWay Platform Policies

## General Terms
1. All projects require clear scope documentation
2. Payments held in escrow until completion
3. Disputes resolved via AI mediation (binding if accepted by both parties)

## Project Types

### Web Development
- Complete functionality: 70% of payment
- UI/UX quality: 20% 
- Documentation: 10%

### Graphic Design
- Concept match: 50%
- Technical specs: 30%
- Delivery time: 20%

### Content Writing
- Word count: 30%
- Quality: 50%
- SEO: 20%

## Dispute Resolution Standards
- Late delivery: Max 20% penalty unless catastrophic
- Incomplete work: Prorated based on completion
- Quality disputes: Require 3 revision cycles before penalty
